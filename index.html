A little about how I work

I'm a local SEO specialist based in Valencia City, Bukidnon, 8709, PH, with seven years spent in the unglamorous but high-leverage part of local search: making sure a business's name, address, and phone number are exactly right, everywhere they appear online.

Most of my work centers on citation building and cleanup — auditing existing listings, fixing NAP inconsistencies, submitting to the directories that actually move rankings, and managing Google Business Profiles for single-location shops and 50-location franchises alike.

I care about the boring accuracy work that most agencies skip, because it's usually the reason a business isn't showing up in the map pack in the first place. Outside of work I run local-SEO workshops for small business owners in my area.
